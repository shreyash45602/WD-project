Our Story

Founded in Kolhapur, Maharashtra, iGAP Technologies Pvt. Ltd. has been at the forefront of enterprise software development for over a decade. What started as a small team of passionate developers has grown into a full-service digital transformation company trusted by organizations across India and beyond.

We specialize in building enterprise ERPs, AI & Data Science solutions, e-commerce ecosystems, and custom digital transformation platforms for global industries. Our deep domain expertise spans healthcare, FMCG, steel trading, and education sectors.

With 10+ years of excellence and 50+ enterprise projects delivered, we combine technical depth with business understanding to create solutions that truly move the needle for our clients.
